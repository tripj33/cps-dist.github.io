The dangers of a flooded basement

A real danger to your home & your family's health

A flooded basement can be a major headache for a homeowner. It can cause damage to your belongings and the structure of your home, and it can also create a breeding ground for mold and other harmful bacteria. Additionally, cleaning up a flooded basement can be a time-consuming and labor-intensive process. It's important to act quickly to mitigate the damage and prevent further problems.

Basement Flooding By the Numbers

Basement flooding can be a major financial drain on homeowners. The repair costs for damage to the foundation, walls, and floors of a home can be significant. It's crucial to take preventative measures to avoid a basement flood and minimize the financial risk.
